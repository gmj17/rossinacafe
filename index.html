Our Story

Where every visit feels like home

Rossina Cafe was born from a love of beautifully made coffee, freshly baked goods, and spaces that invite you to linger. What started as a single café in McAllen has grown into a local chain — but the spirit remains the same.

Whether you're settling in for a study session, catching up with friends, or grabbing your morning strawberry matcha, you'll find warmth in every corner — from the velvet booths to the vintage-framed art.
